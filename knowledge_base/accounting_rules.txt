Office supplies under Rs.5000 with GST are Approved.
Office supplies under Rs.1000 are always Approved regardless of GST.
Invoices between Rs.1000 and Rs.10000 with valid GST are Approved.
Invoices between Rs.1000 and Rs.10000 without GST need Needs Review.
Invoices above Rs.10000 always need Needs Review for manager approval.
Unknown vendors with invoices above Rs.10000 are High risk and Needs Review.
Vendors without GST registration are non-compliant and Needs Review.
Duplicate invoice numbers from same vendor within 30 days are Rejected.
Invoices with incorrect GST calculation are Rejected.
Travel expenses without justification document are Needs Review.
Meal expenses above Rs.500 per person without declaration are Needs Review.
Office supplies below Rs.5000 from recognized vendors are Approved.
